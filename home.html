FAQ’s

What is SchoolMate and who is it for?
SchoolMate is a comprehensive school management and parent communication platform designed for administrators, teachers, and parents to manage academics, attendance, reports, and more — all in one place.
Is SchoolMate available for both web and mobile?
Yes. SchoolMate can be accessed via a web portal and a mobile app available on both Android and iOS devices.
What key features does SchoolMate offer?
  • Attendance Management
  • Marks & Results
  • Study Material Sharing
  • Exam Timetable
  • Notifications & Alerts
  • Student Performance Reports
  • Parent-Teacher Communication
  • More..
How secure is my school’s data on SchoolMate?
SchoolMate uses end-to-end encryption, secure cloud hosting, and regular backups to ensure your school’s data is protected and private
Can parents track their child’s performance and attendance?
Yes. Parents receive real-time updates on attendance, marks, exam schedules, homework, and school announcements via the app.
How easy is it for teachers to update attendance and marks?
Extremely easy. Teachers can log in from their mobile or computer, update daily attendance, enter marks, and upload materials in just a few clicks
Can SchoolMate be customized for our school’s needs?
Yes. SchoolMate offers modular customization to suit your school’s structure, branding, and academic workflows
Does SchoolMate support multi-branch or group schools?
Yes. SchoolMate is built to handle multi-branch institutions with centralized monitoring and individual branch access.
How long does it take to implement SchoolMate in our school?
Full setup and training typically take 7–10 working days, depending on the size and requirements of the school.
What kind of support and training do you provide?
We provide comprehensive onboarding, staff training, and ongoing support via phone, email, and WhatsApp.
